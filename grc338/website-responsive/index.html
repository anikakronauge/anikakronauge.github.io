Reservations

Vesta takes limited reservations, while reserving a portion of the restaurant for walk-ins. Walk-ins are seated on a first come, first serve basis through a waitlist maintained by the host at the front door.

While we offer reservations inside the restaurant for groups up to 12 people, the times at which we offer these reservations are especially limited.

Please call the restaurant for reservations 650-362-5052.

Vesta also offers private gatherings for groups of up to 26 on our back patio space. For any private gathering inquiries, please email patio@vestarwc.com.
